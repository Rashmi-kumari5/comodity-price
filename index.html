News plays a crucial role in commodity price prediction due to its ability to influence market sentiment, supply, demand, and geopolitical factors. Timely and relevant news impacts commodity markets, affecting prices significantly. News acts as a catalyst for market movements, guiding investors and analysts in understanding trends and anticipating price fluctuations. For instance, geopolitical tensions might disrupt the supply of oil, leading to immediate price spikes.
